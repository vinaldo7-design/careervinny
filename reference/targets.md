# Targets (runtime spec)

## Seniority
Mid-level, ~5 yrs post-undergrad. Entry rung = Consultant / Manager / Lead /
Strategist. NOT Head-of / Director / VP yet. Hired AND stretched at this rung.

## HARD GATES (fail any → auto-reject; boolean)
- **UK Skilled Worker visa sponsorship.** Check public register of licensed
  sponsors. Three outcomes — see fit-rubric.md visa logic: PASS / REJECT /
  FLAG-uncertain (only when fit already high).
- **Comp floor: £60k base, GBP.** Below £60k stated = reject. Above £60k is NOT a
  gate — a graduated penalty ranks it (steep near £60–80k, ~zero by £100k+); £60–80k
  roles are KEPT and scored, never silently dropped. Curve: fit-rubric.md §4. (D026)
- **In-person, London** (or London-anchored). Not remote.
- **Strategic, not IC-engineering.** Production-code roles fail even if titled
  "strategist" (see anti-fit + IC tells in rubric).

## PRESTIGE — a MULTIPLIER + band-router, NOT a gate (L004)
- **Prestige.** No longer a gate (was near-gate, demoted L004). A marquee name BOOSTS a
  role and routes high-fit / low-reachability roles to the moonshot/network band (D019);
  its absence never sinks a role. The real protective gate prestige stood for is **firm
  stability + size + security** (successful + large enough to be secure = pass;
  sub-Series-C / shaky = fail). Frontier labs (Anthropic/OpenAI/peers) count — name carries.

## CO-DOMINANT WEIGHTS (a role must score on BOTH)
- **Frontier tech-domain strategy.** Cutting-edge AI strategy advisory matched to
  CS+DS+Oxford-DipAI profile. Frontier of the strategy conversation, not the
  codebase. Scored from observable proxies (see rubric), low-confidence, verify
  live. **Wins the tiebreak over ladder when the two conflict — but only among
  already-prestige options.**
- **Management ladder → people leadership** within ~2-3 yrs. Legible rungs, real
  reports reachable. Heavily optimised for; loses the tiebreak to frontier only
  when both options are already prestige.

## HEAVY WEIGHTS
- Player-coach design (craft now, team now, shed craft later).
- Intellectual agency (judgment shapes work; verify live, low-confidence).
- Peer-bar over comfort.
- Travel actively wanted; road-warrior a plus.
- Client/customer-facing.

## BONUS
- **ESG × AI intersection** → explicit multiplier (see rubric). Highest-leverage,
  rare, high-conversion. Hunt even though rare. Must not be buried by generic AI.

## DISCOUNTS / ANTI-FIT (see rubric anti-fit section for penalties)
- Pure-IC however senior/shiny (oracle trap). Technical/eng/production-code.
- Founder-load / outcome-ownership. Deck-production / no-judgment roles.
- Prestigious-but-dull (cost-takeout, rebadged digital transformation).
- Sub-Series-C startups.

## OUT OF SCOPE (not scored at all)
Ownership-of-function. Coding depth.

## TARGET ROLE FAMILIES (ranked)
1. Customer-facing AI strategy advisory — AI Strategy Consultant/Manager, AI
   Transformation Lead, Applied AI Strategist (QuantumBlack, BCG X, Accenture,
   Deloitte AI; big-tech strategy arms; labs' GTM). Bullseye.
2. Customer-facing tech-strategy at AI/cloud vendors — Strategic/Enterprise CSM
   (AI), AI Solutions Strategist, Deployment *Strategist* (NOT FDE-coding-job).
   Verify it's strategy, not build.
3. Product/Programme strategy with AI mandate — AI PM (strategy-leaning), AI
   Adoption/Enablement Lead.
4. ESG × AI strategy — Responsible AI Lead, AI & Sustainability Strategy. Rare,
   highest-leverage, bonus-weighted.

Industry — any.
